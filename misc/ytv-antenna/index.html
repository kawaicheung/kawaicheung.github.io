WhyTV

A Chrome extension for YouTubeTV

Remember antenna TV? You'd plug it in, mess with the rabbit ears, push ON, then turn the dial. Ca-chunk. Channel 2. Ca-chunk. Ca-chunk. Ca-chunk. Channel 5.

No latency. No buffering. No delayed button feedback. No wondering whether EXIT brings you back to the menu or out of the app altogether. Just immediate live TV.

This is WhyTV, a Chrome extension for your YouTubeTV account. An attempt to bring back TV as it was. 50% nostalgia? Yes. But also 50% “wait, this might be better!”

Install it now. Watch my quick tutorial. And if you like this little thing, you might enjoy some of my other work too.

Handcoded Baseball Scoreboards

A side project by Ka Wai Cheung

I love the look and feel of old-time baseball scoreboards. I also love to create things with a limited set of tools. So, here's my attempt at combining these two interests using only HTML and modern-day CSS. No images. No javascript. Why? Why not?

  • "Two for Friday", 1990 Not a scoreboard but a replica of my first baseball tickets

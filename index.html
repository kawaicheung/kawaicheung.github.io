Handcoded Scoreboards

A side project by Ka Wai Cheung

I've always appreciated the design and majesty of baseball scoreboards, particularly in eras before my time. I also love to create things with a limited set of tools. So, here's my attempt at combining these two interests using only HTML and modern-day CSS. No images. No javascript.

  • Wrigley Field, 1941
  • Comiskey Park, 1978 (In progress)
  • Fenway Park, 2017 (In progress)
